The solution involves removing the incorrect nesting of the <details> elements.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
  <p>This is some text.</p>
  <div>
    <p>This is more text.</p>
    <ul>
      <li>List item 1</li>
      <li>List item 2</li>
    </ul>
    <p>This text is outside the nested details element</p>
  </div>
</details>

By removing the inner <details> tag, the HTML becomes semantically correct and renders consistently across browsers.
